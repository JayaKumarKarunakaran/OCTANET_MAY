About Us

Jai & CO, founded by Jayakumar in 2024, began as an normal idea and has since evolved into a global technology giant, transforming how we shop, read, and consume media.It is an American multinational technology company that focuses on e-commerce, cloud computing,and digital streaming. Jai & CO has been referred to as “one of the most influential economic and cultural forces in the world” and is one of the world’s most valuable brands. Initially, it operated as an online marketplace for Created personal Websit's, but it has expanded into a multitude of product categories, earning it the moniker “The Everything Store”. Beyond e-commerce, Jai &CO subsidiaries include Amazon Web Services (cloud computing), Zoox (autonomous vehicles), Kuiper Systems (satellite Internet), Amazon Lab126 (computer hardware R&D), and more. Its impact on the digital landscape is immense, and its journey from a garage startup to a global powerhouse is nothing short of remarkable.
